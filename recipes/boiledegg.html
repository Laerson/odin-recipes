Boiled Eggs

Description

Boiled Eggs are a easy, fast, healthy and tasty recipe. This is how one should prepare them

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Vinegar

Steps

  1. Put enough water in a pan, so that it can cover the eggs, and heat it.
  2. When the water starts boiling, put the eggs inside the pan, and let it boil for 10~12 minutes
  3. Add a pinch of salt, and a bit of vinegar(the vinegar helps peeling the egg's shell)
  4. Take the eggs out of the boiling water, and put them under cold water for 5 minutes.
  5. Crack the shells and peel the eggs. It is ready
